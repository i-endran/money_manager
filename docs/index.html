Pocket Log

A personal finance tracker for Android & iOS. Fully offline, zero tracking, open source.

Open Source · GPL v3

Features

📅

Month-view Ledger

Day-grouped transactions with a unified month selector and carry-forward balance.

💸

Transactions

Add, edit, and delete expenses, income, and atomic transfers across accounts.

🏦

Multi-account

Bank, Cash, Card, Wallet with nested reserve accounts and closed-box support.

🗂️

3-level Categories

Full parent → child → grandchild category hierarchy with emoji icons.

🎨

Themes

Light, Dark, and System Default theme profiles with a Navy Blue accent.

📦

Import & Export

Back up and restore your data as CSV or XLSX files at any time.

🔒

Biometric Lock

PIN + Face ID / Touch ID lock screen. Auto-locks when sent to background.

💱

Dynamic Currency

Choose your currency symbol — applied everywhere in the app instantly.

🛡️

Your data never leaves your device

No accounts. No internet required. No analytics. No servers. Everything is stored locally in an on-device SQLite database — only you have access to it.
